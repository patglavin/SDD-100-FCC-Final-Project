Early Life

Grace was born in New York City on December 9th, 1906.

Always curious,from a young age she developed a habit of taking apart clocks to see how they worked.

She quickly moved through her education, attending Vassar College at age 17, and later earning both a Masters and a Ph.D. from Yale.
